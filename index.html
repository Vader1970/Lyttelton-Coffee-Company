welcome to lyttelton coffee company

the best little cafe in town

Situated in the heart of the quaint portside town of Lyttelton and set amongst the historic buildings and the landing port for the four settler ships that arrived in New Zealand in 1850, we strive to present a wholy unique Cafe dining experience for our guests.

best views in port

We have the best views of the hustle and bustle of the Lyttelton port on offer, or you can simply relax and view the beauty of the surrounding Banks Peninsula.

everyday specials

We have a wide variety of food to enjoy with daily specials right from the kitchen, made by the best little cafe chef in town.

entertainment on tap

With music gigs, life drawings, artwork, spinclasses...and oh have we mentioned the best views?...we pride ourselves on being one of the most popular destinations in port.

we roast our own coffee

The roaster has moved topside and sits proudly in the middle of the cafe. Roasting most nights of the week, Lyttelton Coffee Co supplies around 30 wholesale clients in the Canterbury region
